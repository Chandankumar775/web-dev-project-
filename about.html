Who We Are

Chandan Ka Dhaba is not just a restaurant — it's a cozy, modern café-dhaba fusion where comfort meets flavor.

We're all about good vibes, great hospitality, and food that actually feels like home. Our founding team believes in simplicity, creativity, and customer-first experiences.

From traditional Indian dishes to modern café favorites, we serve everything with love and authenticity. Whether you're stopping by for a quick bite or staying overnight in our comfortable rooms, we ensure every moment is memorable.

Our Leadership

Meet the people behind Chandan Ka Dhaba

Chandan Kumar

Owner & Founder

Chandan Kumar is the visionary behind "Chandan Ka Dhaba." Focused on quality, experience, and consistency, he drives the overall brand strategy, customer experience, and creative direction of the restaurant.

Krish Garg

Co-Owner

Krish brings a balanced mix of discipline, curiosity, and passion to the team. He believes in constant growth — mentally, physically, and academically. With strong interests in problem-solving, communication, leadership, creativity, and fitness, Krish plays a key role in keeping the dhaba's operations dynamic and future-ready.

Yashoda Thapa

Manager

The heart of our operations, Yashoda ensures that every customer enjoys a warm, smooth, and memorable experience. She is deeply curious and loves learning new things. With a strong creative side in painting and art, she brings a calm, expressive, and aesthetic touch to the dhaba. Music inspires and motivates her during work.
